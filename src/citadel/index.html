The Citadel

The Citadel is an enormous metal structure that towers above City 17. It is just one of numerous citadels scattered across the earth. These citadels serve as the headquarters for the Combine's presence on Earth, an inter-dimensional empire that conquered Earth in the early 21th century.

The Citadel in City 17 resembles a massive metallic skyscraper stretching approximately 3.4 kilometers into the sky. It is the most important of the Combine's citadels on Earth, housing the office of Earth's administrator, Dr. Wallace Breen, and serving as the central hub for the Combine's operations on the planet. It also contains a Dark Energy Fusion Reactor at the bottom of the structure which provides the energy to create portals to the Combine homeworld.

The Citadel is heavily mechanised, it is surrounded by Combine Smart Barriers, very large mechanical walls which can be hoisted up to "walk" forward in an often destructive display. The structure itself also contains many similar mechanical components, allowing it to transform to facilitate mass deployment of Combine air forces. Most notably, a large section of the Citadel's shielding can be retracted to reveal an array of rib-like structures.

Half Life 2 and its episodes are set in and around the Citadel, and it is a major location in the games.
